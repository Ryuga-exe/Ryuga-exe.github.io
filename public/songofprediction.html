Song of Perdition
May 18, 2022

Welcome to v5.7 Song of Perdition!
"If it were possible... I'd rather use these hands to soothe your aches... and doubts." New S-rank battlesuit Disciplinary Perdition debuts!
"I'm just a singer who shines on stage." New SP battlesuit Golden Diva debuts!

Chapter XXIX: Tochter aus Elysium is now available alongside Open World Elysium Everlasting and new gameplay Weapon Cards! Play the event to get Crystals, Bringer of Light Stigma Option, Golden Diva Fragments and more! Login event of v5.7 rewards High Baroque Maestro Stigma Option, Supply Cards and more. Complete v5.7 event missions to obtain Crystals, Honkai Shards, Einstein's Torus and more in event shop Immortal Hymn.

Update Content
◆ New BIO-type S-rank Battlesuit Disciplinary Perdition A BIO-type S-rank ranged battlesuit who deals Lightning DMG. She uses Chakram as a ring in battle to attack enemies.
*You can obtain the new battlesuits in the following ways:
After the v5.7 update until 12:00, JUN 17, Disciplinary Perdition will be available in Expansion Supply! You can also get the UP weapon Lost Conviction and Paradoxical Philosophy stigma set Zeno recommended for her from the concurrent Expansion Equipment Supply and Focused Supply!

◆ New IMG-type SP Battlesuit Golden Diva An IMG-type ranged battlesuit who deals Lightning DMG, and with her flintlock pistols, she plays music on the battlefield in a unique style, providing support to allies.

*You can obtain the new battlesuits in the following ways:
After the v5.7 update, complete certain Open World Adventure Tasks to earn Golden Diva Fragments.
From 04:00, MAY 23, Golden Diva Fragments will be sold in the Asterite Shop and Battle Arsenal for Asterite and Gold Pins respectively.
In v5.7, participate in Tochter aus Elysium and Shattered Dimensions events to get Golden Diva Fragments .
After the v5.7 update and until 12:00, JUN 24, Fluorescent Cat Balls can be used to exchange for Golden Diva Fragments in Felistore.
After the v5.7 update and until 12:00, JUN 3, you are guaranteed to get Golden Diva in Supply event Golden Melody within 25 drops. Meanwhile, you are guaranteed to get Golden Diva's recommended UP weapon Echo of Paradise in the tie-in Equipment Supply within 50 drops!
After the v5.7 update and until 04:00, JUN 6, you can log in to receive High Baroque Maestro Stigma Option x1.
After the v5.7 update, stigmata High Baroque Maestro will become directly craftable.

◆ Story Chapter XXIX: Tochter aus Elysium Elysium Everlasting is a secret space hidden in the recesses of Elysian Realm. All the truths are about to be uncovered, and Mei has finally made one step closer to the final answers...
Open World Elysium Everlasting released! New feature Weapon Cards debuts! Play to get Crystals, Bringer of Light Stigma Option, Golden Diva Fragments and more!
The lip-sync of certain Valkyries will go online in v5.8. Please follow our official announcements to learn more. We are sorry for any inconvenience caused!

◆ Login Event
"Please" step ahead, as the strings of destiny have revealed the end.
In v5.7, Captains can log in to get High Baroque Maestro Stigma Option x1, Focused Supply Card x10, SP Supply Card x10 and Golden Diva Fragment x5!

◆ Special Event Shop Immortal Hymn
That long repetitive melody used to be the anthem of the era.
Event shop Immortal Hymn will open after the v5.7 update until 04:00, JUN 30. Complete missions on the event panel of Burning Starlight to exchange for Crystals, Honkai Shards, Einstein's Torus, Ether Fuel and more in event shop Immortal Hymn.

◆ Featured Event Shattered Dimensions
The fractured staircases lead to unknown adventures, and the incomplete notes record the disappeared truths.
In this shattered world, any hesitation will let the shadowy hunter prey on you.
New featured event Shattered Dimensions will be released in v5.7. Play the limited-time event to get Bright Knight: Excelsis's outfit Gale Hunter, Golden Diva Fragments, Crystals, Ancient Willpower and more.

◆ Event Shop Felistore
Felistore opens! Boss, everything here's a steal! Don't miss them out!
After the v5.7 update and until 12:00, JUN 17, Captains can purchase relevant bundles or spend Crystals in Supply events to obtain Fluorescent Cat Balls and exchange them for Reverist Calico's outfit Spectral Claws, Collector's Card and more in Felistore.

◆ Rerun Event Rosemary's Floriograph
Evil mists have shrouded the town of dead silence. She walks close as raindrops hit the ground, and if there were any guidance by the ghost...
In v5.7, Rosemary's Floriograph is being rerun! Participate to get Crystals, Fleurs du Mal Option, Golden Diva Fragments, Ancient Legacies and more!
