World Honkai

Honkai Impact 3 takes place in today's world, but in a version of the world ravaged by natural disasters caused by "Honkai". The word comes from the word "Houkai", which can be roughly translated to "Destruction". Honkai is something that has no mass, but is influenced by its own gravity. When a Honkai sinks into its own gravity, a burst of energy is formed which is known as an "impact"

Valkrye

Valkyries (Japanese: 戦乙女 Sen'otome) are powerful female warriors of Schicksal who have high Honkai resistance and who either have natural or artificial stigmata. Due to the constant exposure to the volatile Honkai energy and Honkai Beasts, many Valkyries are at risk of losing their lives not only in battle but due to the possible Honkai cascade as well, which is a phenomenon when a human with mild adaptability is corrupted by the Honkai and turns into a zombie, mindlessly seeking to destroy humanity.
