v5.4 [Silverwing: Beyond] Update Announcement
Jan 11, 2022

Welcome to v5.4: [Silverwing: Beyond]!
After that catastrophe, the ex-soldier chose to live in peace. But when the world fell into another crisis, the familiar figure reappeared on the battlefield. S-rank battlesuit [Silverwing: N-EX] debuts! New Open World [A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2] released! Take part in the limited-time event to obtain the stigma option recommended for [Silverwing: N-EX]!
Chapter XXIX: Tochter aus Elysium is now available alongside Open World Elysium Everlasting and new gameplay Weapon Cards! Play the event to get Crystals, Bringer of Light Stigma Option, Golden Diva Fragments and more! Login event of v5.7 rewards High Baroque Maestro Stigma Option, Supply Cards and more. Complete v5.7 event missions to obtain Crystals, Honkai Shards, Einstein's Torus and more in event shop Immortal Hymn.

Update Content

◆ New Battlesuit: Silverwing: N-EX S-rank battlesuit [Silverwing: N-EX] debuts: A BIO-type battlesuit capable of dealing Ice DMG. Use Combo ATK or Ultimate to deploy a scatter barrier for burst attacks.
*You can obtain the new battlesuits in the following ways: From the v5.4 update to 12:00, FEB 4, [Silverwing: N-EX] Expansion Supply will be available! [Silverwing: N-EX] drop rate UP!
This round introduces a limited-time plan that rewards specific numbers of drops. You are guaranteed to choose every weapon and stigma recommended for [Silverwing: N-EX] in 190 drops!

◆ New Open World: A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2 The stars, the sun, and the moon shall not give their light. Falling into the submerged ruins from the Arcane Castle, will they find what they seek?
Take part in the limited-time A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2 event to obtain any piece of the stigma set recommended for [Silverwing: N-EX] you desire, Crystals, and Einstein's Torus/Honkai Cube option!

◆ New Story Chapter 28: Beyond Will
The confrontation with Otto induced more enigmas and emergencies. How will they escape? Will Otto's plan come to fruition? Take part in the limited-time Story Chapter 28 event to obtain Umbral Rose's outfit [Valkyrie Dawn], 4★ event stigma, and Crystals!

◆ Outfit Shop New Arrivals [Miss Pink♪], the new outfit for Miss Pink Elf♪, is hitting the Outfit Shop in v5.4. In its launch week, it will be discounted from 1980 B-Chips to 1680 B-Chips! Please follow announcements for the launch date.

◆ Additions to the Hyperion Arsenal New weapons: Heavy cannon [Falcon Flare 0019] and heavy PRI-ARM [Falcon Flare: FINAL].
New stigma set: Wings of Reason set [Bronya: N-EX].

◆ Additions to the Dressing Room
[Miss Pink♪] For Miss Pink Elf♪
[Valkyrie Dawn] for Umbral Rose

Game Adjustments & Optimizations

※※※※※Battlesuits※※※※※

The following data are at max skill level:
◆ Herrscher of Flamescion
1. Fixed an issue where VFX sometimes glitched while she was in the default outfit. 2. Fixed an issue where models or VFX sometimes glitched while she was in outfit [Time Runner].

◆ Miss Pink Elf♪
1. Fixed the glitched animations on the bridge. 2. Fixed an issue where her Charged ATK sometimes couldn't hit enemies. 3. Fixed an issue where her weapon actives sometimes dealt double the damage. 4. Fixed an issue in Co-op where, after she cast [Whisper of the Past: Sonnet]'s active, the crystalline circle it created also slowed her teammates within it.

◆ Herrscher of Sentience
1. Fixed an issue where tapping ULT to switch weapons sometimes made her switch twice. 2. Fixed an issue where her chain blades sometimes recovered energy at the wrong rate while attacking some bosses.

◆ Sweet 'n' Spicy
1. Added the "global Time Fracture" line to her skill [Kissy Pillow ☆ Burst]'s description to match its actual performance.
2. Adjusted her skill [Mood Maker]'s effect to "Ultimate boosts team Physical DMG and team Total DMG for 20s (originally 15s)."
3. Fixed an issue where the stigmata on her back sometimes glitched.
4. Fixed an issue where she didn't benefit from stigma affixes capable of "reducing active skill SP cost."
5. Fixed an issue where her passive skill [Amazing Mode]'s effect of "reducing incoming Total DMG" sometimes didn't work properly.

◆ Midnight Absinthe
1. Fixed an issue where her Ultimate [Nightbird's Call] was sometimes affected by the Frozen state.
2. Fixed an issue where her Ultimate [Nightbird's Call] sometimes had glitched VFX.

◆ Prinzessin der Verurteilung!
1. Fixed an issue where the horizontal arrow VFX of her charged shots sometimes glitched.
2. Fixed an issue where her Ultimate [Midnight Phantasmagoria] sometimes consumed SP without playing the animations nor dealing DMG.

◆ Haxxor Bunny
Fixed an issue where her finalization animations sometimes glitched while she was in outfit [Old Times].
◆ Herrscher of the Void
1. Fixed an issue where her model sometimes glitched while she was in outfit [Magic Girl ☆ Sirin].
2. Fixed an issue where her model sometimes glitched during finalization while she was in outfit [Parasol Kaiserin].

◆ Argent Knight: Artemis
Fixed an issue where the [Crescent Harvests] summoned by her Basic ATK didn't benefit from stigma [Turgenev].

◆ Fervent Tempo Δ, Molotov Cherry
1. Fixed an issue where she could evade while exiting.
2. Fixed an issue where her tail VFX disappeared during finalization while she was in outfit [Gothic Rozamary].

◆ Azure Empyrea
Fixed an issue where her expressions sometimes glitched during bridge interactions while she was in outfit [Taixuan Impression].
◆ Blood Rose
Fixed an issue where her hair glitched while she was showing the stigmata on her back.

◆ Starlit Astrologos
1. Fixed an issue where she may appear outside the battelfield when casting QTE [Battle Summit].
2. Fixed an issue where her QTE sometimes couldn't be triggered.

◆ Luna Kindred
Fixed the glitched stigma decals on her back.

◆ Others
The models of [Valkyrie Triumph], [Battle Storm], and [Holy Rose] are glitched.
