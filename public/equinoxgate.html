v5.5 [Equinox Gate] Update Announcement
Feb 23, 2022

"The Equinox path starts here!" New S-rank battlesuit Palatinus Equinox debuts!
"It is a maid's duty to handle each battle carefully." New S-rank battlesuit Spina Astera debuts!

Event Spring Treasure rewards S-rank battlesuit Spina Astera and an S-rank battlesuit option! Spring events reward Haxxor Bunny's new outfit Arc City Blues, Tiger Roar Emblem, and Crystals! Spring Lobby 2022, A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2, and more exciting content await!


Update Content

◆ New IMG-type S-rank Battlesuit: Palatinus Equinox
IMG-type Physical DMG dealer who can access Guardian and Knight forms freely. In Knight form, she will ride the mighty steed Krishna formed by black armor to trample enemies in a flash!

◆ New MECH-type S-rank Battlesuit: Spina Astera
MECH-type Fire DMG dealer who wields a scythe and a simulated Holy Blade.
*You can obtain the new battlesuits in the following ways: After the v5.5 update until 12:00, MAR 25, Palatinus Equinox will be available in Expansion Supply! You can also pull the UP weapon Midnight Eclipse and the Equinoctial Wonder set Bianka: Theatre (T, M, B) recommended for her from the concurrent Expansion Equipment Supply and Focused Supply!
After the v5.5 update, complete the event Spring Treasure's missions to earn points. You can obtain Spina Astera for free in 4 days at the earliest! You can also pull Spina Astera from EXPA Select Supply!
The Equipment Supply recommended for Spina Astera is available for a limited time in v5.5. Every 10 drops in the first 80 drops will reward Focused Supply Card x5, totaling Focused Supply Card x40. Every 30 drops are guaranteed to contain a NEW UP equipment item; In other words, you are guaranteed to pull all UP equipment items in 120 drops. You can also pull the equipment recommended for Spina Astera from FOCA Select Supply!

◆ First Top-Up Double Reset
After the v5.5 update, your first top-up will give you double Crystals again!

◆ Anniversary Cake Party
After the v5.5 update, join the 4th-anniversary celebration to obtain Anniversary Supply Box x3, Ancient Voice Box x2, and Shining Anniversary Box x3. Open them to choose from tons of Supply Cards, Crystals, and growth materials!

◆ Version Update Login Event
After the v5.5 update, log in for a number of days while the event lasts to obtain Expansion Supply Card x5 and Focused Supply Card x5!

◆ Spring Treasure Rewards the New S-rank Battlesuit
After the version update on FEB 24, complete event missions to accrue points to unlock rewards! After opening Spring Treasure, you can obtain Spina Astera in 4 days and Springtime Option in 6 days at the earliest!

◆ Spring Event: Secret Project: Arahato
After the v5.5 update, play the event to obtain Haxxor Bunny's new outfit Arc City Blues, Reroll Device: ATK Option Type B, Crystals, Tiger Roar Emblem, Ancient Legacy, and Honkai Shard!

◆ Spring Event: Spring Lobby 2022
After the v5.5 update, play the activities Senti Reads Lots, Box Hunt, Spring Feast, Spring Lantern Fair (event phase 2), and Glowing Riddles (event phase 2) in the event to obtain Crystals, Honkai Shard, and Ancient Willpower!

◆ Limited-time Total Spend Bonuses
After the v5.5 update, spend a number of Crystals in Supply events to obtain Dea Anchora's outfit Heavenward Dragon and Spina Astera Rank-up Stamps!

◆ Spring Fireworks Fair Rewards Special Options
After the v5.5 update, complete event missions to obtain Whistle Fireworks, Willow Fireworks, and Jade Fireworks. Spend them to open special options that let you choose from Honkai Shard, Ancient Legacy, Ancient Willpower, Einstein's Torus, and battlesuit fragments.

◆ A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2 Updated
After the v5.5 update, APHO's story continues! New map Arcane Castle, new mode Judgment Falls, and limited-time co-op event Squads Assemble will be available. Stargate exploration, challenge, and co-op stages will be expanded. Mei and Carole will return to the squad. Play the APHO event to obtain Crystals and Asterite!

◆ Outfit Shop New Arrivals
Silverwing: N-EX's new outfit Heart of the Night is hitting the Outfit Shop in v5.5. In its launch week, it will be discounted from 1980 B-Chips to 1680 B-Chips! Please follow announcements for the launch date.

◆ ELF: Blade Durandal
New S-rank ELF Blade Durandal debuts! She can attack rapidly or charge against enemies to deal Physical DMG. Her Ultimate pulls in enemies and follows up with a multi-hit AoE attack. Once it ends, she will pull in enemies again to deal explosive DMG. Her attack stacks a debuff on hit, which makes enemies take more Physical DMG. Her Ultimate also reduces switch CD.

◆ Additions to the Hyperion Arsenal
New weapons: Lance Midnight Eclipse, PRI lance Midnight Ataegina, scythe Astral Rend: White Dwarf, PRI scythe Astral Divide: Pisces join the arsenal!
New stigma sets: Equinoctial Wonder set Bianka: Theatre (T, M, B) and Schicksal Mentor set Ragna (T, M, B) join the arsenal!

◆ Additions to the Dressing Room
Heart of the Night and Neonized for Silverwing: N-EX.
Heavenward Dragon and Red Dragon Rises for Dea Anchora.
Arc City Blues for Haxxor Bunny.
