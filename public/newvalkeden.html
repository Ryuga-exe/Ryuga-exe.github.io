Ai-chan Newsflash: New SP Battlesuit Golden Diva Debuts

May 6, 2022

Ai-chan returns alongside the new version! Today's episode is all about the new SP battlesuit Golden Diva! Read on to learn more about her!
Let's check out the unique gaming experience brought by each of the two!

※※※※ Golden Diva Files ※※※※

I'm but an entertainer, a star that shines for the audience.

Name: Eden
Affiliation: The Thirteen Flame-Chasers
Ranking: 4th
Weapon: Pistols
Signet: Gold

She is the symbol of that era, a superstar shining the whole world. Model, artist, musician, hostess, and designer... she lived a very colorful life in the old world.
She is also a MANTIS fighting for humanity. Though she treads the path of sunset, her singing brings the last hope to the people living in the despair of doomsday

※※※※ Skill Showcase ※※※※

Golden Diva is an IMG-type ranged SP battlesuit that deals Lightning DMG and attacks with pistols. The moves of Golden Diva are elegant and dynamic, and with her flintlock pistols, she plays music on the battlefield in unique style, providing support to allies. After casting Ultimate, she gently plucks the harp strings to play the gripping Century Chapter that enhances allies.

◆ Basic ATK: Caprice: Starlight
Golden Diva battles with pistols. Her Basic ATK consists of three sequences, with each accumulating one music note for the current Bar. Meanwhile, Golden Diva's Basic ATK gets enhanced as the accumulation progresses. She deals Lightning DMG to enemies through Basic ATK.

◆ Combo ATK: Romance: Paradise
Golden Diva completes her Performance on the battlefield in a unique style. The Score of Performance has two Bars, with up to three music notes for each Bar. When the First Bar or the Second Bar reaches maxed music notes, she can finish the Bar with Combo ATK Chord or Combo ATK Variation. When Performance is done for the two Bars, Score will be activated and then you can hold [ATK] to consume all music notes for unleashing Ensemble: Golden Diva hurls pistols to the front and the weapons quickly spin to attack enemies by dealing Lightning DMG and inflicting Paralyze Trauma.

◆ SP Skill: Unique Singing When Golden Diva consumes 6 music notes to unleash Ensemble, a golden halo will appear on her body. Seize the moment to release [ATK] to consume all remaining music notes for unleashing Ensemble Playing, which deals Lightning DMG and inflicts Paralyze Trauma.

◆ Ultimate: Fantasy: Century Golden Diva plucks harp strings to play Century Chapter that enhances allies and applies Sound of Nature to herself. Meanwhile, Sound of Nature also restores all her music notes, activates Score, and allows notes in the current Bar to quickly charge for faster Performance. The generated sound waves will spread to attack enemies by dealing Lightning DMG and inflicting Paralyze Trauma

※※※※ How to Get the Recommended Gear ※※※※

In v5.7, Captains can obtain Golden Diva's recommended stigma set Handel by through Supply events and the following two channels:
◆ [Login] After the v5.7 update, Captains can participate in the login event to obtain High Baroque Maestro Stigma Option x1. Use the stigma option to obtain one of the following stigmata: Handel Stigma (T), Handel Stigma (M), or Handel Stigma (B).

◆ [Foundry] Handel can be obtained by transmuting Ishikawa Goemon in corresponding positions in the Foundry! The materials required for transmutation are as follows:
*New material: Pure Purple Crystalyte
After the v5.7 update, G4 Lightning DMG stigma crafting material Pure Purple Crystalyte, which can be obtained by clearing the newly added Universal Mirage: Roaring Palace's 7F. Pure Purple Crystalyte will also be listed in Mirage Store.

*Stigma Ishikawa Goemon
After the v5.7 update, stigma set Ishikawa Goemon will become directly craftable.
