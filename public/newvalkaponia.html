Ai-chan Newsflash: New S-rank Battlesuit Disciplinary Perdition Debuts

May 8, 2022

Greetings, Captains! The star of today's episode is the new battlesuit Disciplinary Perdition! Read on to learn more about her!
Let's check out the unique gaming experience brought by each of the two!

※※※※ Disciplinary Perdition Files ※※※※

"If it were possible... I'd rather use these hands to soothe your aches... and doubts."

Name: Aponia
Affiliation: The Thirteen Flame-Chasers
Ranking: 3rd
Weapon: Chakram
Signet: Discipline

Destiny bestows on her a merciful heart, but her tenderness and comfort never cured or saved anyone...
She is above everyone else to watch over every moment of destiny. By consigning herself to the confinement called the Deep End, she takes the place of humanity to bear all the sins in the world.

※※※※ Skill Showcase ※※※※

Disciplinary Perdition is a BIO-type S-rank ranged battlesuit that deals Lightning DMG and wields a Chakram in battle. It's in gentleness and serenity that Disciplinary Perdition exudes the sense of danger and mystery. She uses Chakram as a ring to attack enemies. She can switch between two forms: Conviction and Predictor's Sentence. With the Chakram she can be airborne to support allies from mid-air. Once fully charged, she can strike surrounding enemies with Thunderstorms. When casting Ultimate, she rises into the sky and delivers punishment to all enemies, bringing in the destined trial for sinners.

◆ Basic ATK: Love and Hate, Discerned Disciplinary Perdition uses the Chakram as a ring in battle to attack enemies. Disciplinary Perdition's Basic ATK consists of four sequences. In the third and the fourth, Disciplinary Perdition will summon a huge ring in the air to bombard enemies and restore Redemption. Her Basic ATK deals Lightning DMG to enemies.

◆ Combo ATK: Good and Evil, Absolute
Without full Redemption, using weapon skill sends Disciplinary Perdition into Conviction form and restores Redemption. In such form, Disciplinary Perdition uses the Chakram to become airborne, and you can hold [ATK] to attack enemies with Thunderstorms. You can also switch another Valkyrie in while she provides support from mid-air, and her Lightning DMG will be added to the Lightning DMG dealt by the teammate on the ground. When Redemption is full and Disciplinary Perdition is still on the field, she will exit and reset her switch CD.

When Redemption is full, you can hold [ATK] to send Disciplinary Perdition into Predictor's Sentence form. In such form, Disciplinary Perdition strikes surrounding enemies with Thunderstorms. When Redemption is reset to zero, Disciplinary Perdition will unleash Inhibitor's Adjudication that deals Lightning DMG, and she will exit Predictor's Sentence form.

◆ Ultimate: This Sin, Inexorable
Disciplinary Perdition rises into the sky and delivers punishment to all enemies, bringing in the destined trial for sinners and dealing multi-stage Lightning DMG, during which all skill timers and stage timers are paused.
After the v5.7 update, Disciplinary Perdition will have Bridge interactions and voicelines.
