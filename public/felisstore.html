Ai-chan's Newsflash: Felistore Opens

May 12, 2022

Hi there. Now Ai-chan is glad to introduce Felistore, a place filled with good stuff you can't miss! Come and take a look now!

※※※※※ Felistore ※※※※※

Felistore will open after the new version is released. Don't miss it out!
During the event, you can purchase relevant bundles or spend Crystals in Supply events to obtain Fluorescent Cat Balls and
exchange them for Reverist Calico's outfit Spectral Claws, Collector's Card, and more in Felistore.

※※※※※ Event Items ※※※※※

※ Outfit Spectral Claws will later return for 1,680 B-Chips. Outfit Option which offers Orchid's Night, Neon Shade, Old Times, or Summer Night's Dream is limited to one purchase per Captain.

※ During the event, Captains can use Fluorescent Cat Balls to exchange for up to two Collector's Cards. Use them to exchange for equipment in Collector's Shop (including select 4★ supply gear from v3.5 to v4.9). Only a piece of a stigma set can be exchanged for.

※※※※※ How to Get Fluorescent Cat Balls ※※※※※

Next, please allow me to introduce Fluorescent Cat Balls, the core currency in Felistore.

During the event, you can purchase relevant bundles or spend Crystals in Supply events to obtain Fluorescent Cat Balls. Below are the detailed rules:
1. Spend a number of Supply Cards in Supply events (except for Dorm Supply): Using every 2,800 Crystals' worth of Supply Cards rewards Fluorescent Cat Ball x40 (caps at 520, which is equal to 36,400 Crystals' worth of Supply Cards). After you spent 40,000 Crystals' worth of Supply Cards, you will obtain additional Fluorescent Cat Ball x60.

2. Spend a number of Supply Cards in Dorm or Equipment Supply: Using every 2,000 Crystals' worth of Supply Cards rewards Fluorescent Cat Ball x25 (caps at 50).
*Ai-chan's Reminder: You can obtain up to Fluorescent Cat Ball x630 from all kinds of Supply events. The total spending of Supply Cards and Crystals in different Supply types is added together. For example, you can work towards milestones by spending Focused/Expansion Supply Cards. 3. Themed Bundles in Mall: Special bundles that contain Fluorescent Cat Balls will be available in Mall during the event. Please refer to the bundle announcement and the Mall screen for details. The schedules of Supply events will be released later via the calendar on social media. Stay tuned! ※ The above info is taken from the test server and is subject to change in the live version.
